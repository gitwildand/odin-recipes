Golden Toast

Description

A simple yet perfect thick-cut toast, scored for extra crunch and finished with rich melted butter and a sweet drizzle of honey.

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread (thick cut): 1 slice
  • Butter: 10g
  • Honey: To taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your toaster.
  2. Cut a shallow cross-hatch pattern into the surface of the bread.
  3. Toast for about 3 minutes until golden brown.
  4. Spread the butter while hot and finish with a drizzle of honey.
